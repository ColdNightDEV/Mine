STRUCTURE

Outerverse Vessels will consist of two NFT mints.

The first one will be a Blueprint Pass. By owning your pass, you will be eligible of minting your complete vessel.

The second drop will be your Outerverse Vessel NFT. Giving you the ability to explore your Vessel and The Outerverse open world.

To read more about structure, mint details, and much more, visit the Outerverse Vessels whitepaper (at the bottom of the website).

SUPPLY-1750

The supply will consist of 1750 NFTs. These NFTs will be in the form of passes. The supply will be allocated for pre-sale and public sale.

MINT PRICE 0.2 ETH

All PASSES will be minted at the price of 0.2 ETH per wallet. Buyers will have the chance at minting a maximum of 2 PASSES per wallet.

PRE-SALE

There will be 750 total whitelist spots. Any unminted PASSES will be reserved for a public sale. Whitelisted individuals will have 24 hours to mint their PASSES before the remaining supply goes to the public sale.
